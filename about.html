About

Hi, I'm Chad.

Over the last 25 years, I've helped companies like Cisco-WebEx, Riverbed Technology, ON24, ConnectAndSell, RingCentral, and Informatica (a Salesforce company) launch world-class virtual sales and business development teams — and I founded BDR.ai to give sales professionals and entrepreneurs a competitive edge. I'm the host of the AI for Sales Podcast and the author of 9 books.

Home base is Denver, Colorado — where I founded the local AA-ISP chapter and where I'm currently exploring my next SDR/BDR leadership role. What gets me up in the morning is simple: building teams that hit numbers, and building people who become leaders.

Away from work, I'm thankful for my family, my friends, and my faith — something my Grandpa Harold and Grandma Marian instilled in me at a very young age.

God's peace, Chad
