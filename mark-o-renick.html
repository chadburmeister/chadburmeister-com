Be Extraordinary Groups · Week 2

Mark O’Renick

#FLOW · 7:55

▶  Watch and Listen

Summary

Mark O’Renick discusses the challenges he faced when people told him he couldn't do certain things. He sees these obstacles as opportunities and a chance to prove others wrong. He also talks about the concept of "state of liminality," where people struggle to visualize a world where profit and purpose can coexist. Mark emphasizes the importance of overcoming fear, uncertainty, and doubt, and finding a way forward.

Chad shares his own experience with facing challenges and breaking through barriers. He talks about the need for "border breakers" who can help push past the naysayers and encourage taking risks. Chad also discusses the idea of living in flow rather than seeking balance and how he follows God's calling in his pursuit of his purpose. He shares his commitment to making a positive impact through his business and helping others in need.

Appropriate Bible Verses

Philippians 4:13 (NIV): "I can do all this through him who gives me strength."

Proverbs 16:9 (NIV): "In their hearts, humans plan their course, but the Lord establishes their steps."

Isaiah 41:10 (NIV): "So do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God. I will strengthen you and help you; I will uphold you with my righteous right hand."

James 4:13-15 (NIV): "Now listen, you who say, 'Today or tomorrow we will go to this or that city, spend a year there, carry on business and make money.' Why, you do not even know what will happen tomorrow.

Psalm 46:10 (NIV): "He says, 'Be still, and know that I am God; I will be exalted among the nations, I will be exalted in the earth.'"

Group Discussion

How do you personally relate to the idea of facing challenges and obstacles in your life, much like Mark's experience of being told he couldn't do something? Can you share a specific instance where you encountered such a challenge and how it transformed your life for the better?

Chad mentioned the concept of "border breakers" or individuals who help us break through barriers and obstacles in our path. Who has been a "border breaker" in your life, and how did their encouragement or support make a significant difference in your journey?

Both Mark and Chad discussed the importance of following God's calling and living in the flow of life rather than seeking balance. How has your faith or spirituality played a role in guiding your life's purpose and helping you navigate obstacles and opportunities?
