Be Extraordinary Groups · Week 2

Sati Hillyer

#SEND ME · 6:09

Watch and Listen

Summary

Sati Hillyer shares his personal experiences and how they have shaped his perspective on life. Growing up in a single-parent household, he faced challenges but also realized the power and potential within himself. He learned that taking risks and embracing opportunities, even if they seemed impractical at the time, can lead to personal growth and appreciation for the present moment. Sati also discusses the role of faith in his life, emphasizing the importance of living by example and embodying the teachings of faith in his daily actions.

Overall, Sati's journey has taught him the value of overcoming struggles, appreciating the present, and helping others through their own challenges. He believes that the integration of different cultures, religions, and ideas is a powerful aspect of humanity, and strives to live a life of openness, inclusion, and giving.

Group Discussion

Question on Coping with Trauma and Adversity:

Discussion Question: Sati shared his experience of dealing with life's challenges and how they have become part of his journey. Can you think of a time when you faced adversity or a painful memory that, in retrospect, contributed to your personal growth? How did you handle it, and how does it continue to influence your life today?

Relevant Bible Verse: Romans 8:28 (NIV) - "And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose."

Application to Life: Explore the concept that even in challenging times, God's plan may be at work for our good, as suggested in the provided Bible verse. Share your own stories of overcoming adversity and how they have shaped your beliefs and choices.

2. Question on Finding Purpose Through Life's Challenges:

Discussion Question: Sati discussed how life's experiences have led his to focus on helping others discover their purpose. How do you perceive the role of life's challenges and experiences in shaping your sense of purpose? Have you ever felt a calling to help others based on your personal history or skills?

Relevant Bible Verse: Proverbs 19:21 (NIV) - "Many are the plans in a person's heart, but it is the Lord's purpose that prevails."

Application to Life: Reflect on how life's challenges can redirect our plans and lead us to a greater purpose, as indicated in the provided Bible verse. Share insights into how your experiences have influenced your life's mission and your desire to make a positive impact.

3. Question on Faith as a Guiding Force:

Discussion Question: Sati emphasized the importance of faith and seeking guidance from spiritual teachings in his daily life. How do you incorporate your faith into your daily decision-making processes? What role does faith play in your personal and professional journey?

Relevant Bible Verse: Proverbs 3:5-6 (NIV) - "Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your understanding; in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight."

Application to Life: Discuss the significance of trusting in God's guidance, as suggested in the provided Bible verse. Share examples of how your faith has influenced your decision-making and how it has helped you navigate life's challenges.
