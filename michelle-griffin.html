Be Extraordinary Groups · Week 4

Michelle Griffin

#PERSEVERE · 7:19

▶  Watch and Listen

Summary

Michelle Griffin shares her personal journey of feeling stuck in her job and searching for her true purpose. Despite feeling called to do something more, she struggled with self-doubt and fear, constantly seeking validation through courses and certifications. Finally, she mustered the courage to quit her job and pursue her passion, becoming an entrepreneur dedicated to helping others find their own path. Michelle also discusses moments where she felt God's presence, experiencing a calm and reassuring feeling that things would work out during times of desperation. She emphasizes the importance of faith in her life and how it guides her actions and decisions, even if she is not perfect in her practice.

Overall, this conversation highlights the transformative power of pushing through challenges, believing in oneself, and relying on faith. It serves as an inspirational reminder that even in the face of uncertainty, it is possible to find clarity and purpose by trusting in a higher power and taking bold steps towards one's calling.

Group Discussion

Reflecting on your own life, can you share a personal experience or challenge that you believe represents the need to complete a past chapter? How did you eventually overcome it, and what did you learn from this process?

In the present moment, what aspects of your life do you believe require recognition or acceptance, whether positive or challenging? How does acknowledging your current situation impact your overall well-being and decision-making?

Considering the lessons learned from both your past experiences and your current situation, how can you apply this knowledge to make intentional choices that shape your future? What steps can you take to actively "choose your future" and work towards your desired goals or aspirations?
