Be Extraordinary Groups · Week 1

Andrea Sitzes

#REBOOT · 7:45

▶  Watch and Listen

Summary

Andrea shares some of her painful memories and how they have shaped her life. She talks about incidents like a childhood accident that resulted in her having to relearn everything, getting run over by a team of horses and a wagon, and experiencing a falling out with a close group of friends in high school. Despite these challenges, Andrea learned to stand on her own and became independent, which set the foundation for her personal growth. She also discusses the role of faith in her life, emphasizing that it is everything to her. She believes that faith is like walking on water and that it provides strength and guidance in both good and desperate times.

Additionally, Andrea mentions a moment in her life where she feels God showed up undeniably for her, which was meeting her husband. Their unexpected connection and the way their stories intertwined have changed the trajectory of her life. She describes her husband as the string to her balloon, keeping her grounded and constant. Andrea concludes by highlighting the importance of faith in keeping her anchored and giving her a true North to navigate through life's challenges.

Appropriate Bible Verses

1 Peter 1:7 - "These have come so that the proven genuineness of your faith—of greater worth than gold, which perishes even though refined by fire—may result in praise, glory, and honor when Jesus Christ is revealed." This verse speaks to the idea that challenges and trials can refine one's faith and character.

Romans 8:28 - "And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose." Andrea's journey and the unexpected meeting with her husband reflect the notion that God can work all things together for good, even in the midst of challenges.

Hebrews 11:1 - "Now faith is confidence in what we hope for and assurance about what we do not see." This verse underscores the importance of faith as the foundation of one's relationship with God, as well as the confidence in unseen blessings and outcomes.

2 Corinthians 12:9 - "But he said to me, 'My grace is sufficient for you, for my power is made perfect in weakness.' Therefore I will boast all the more gladly about my weaknesses, so that Christ’s power may rest on me." Andrea's experiences of facing physical challenges and hardships resonate with the idea that God's grace and strength shine through in moments of weakness.

Matthew 14:29-31 - "He said, 'Come.' So Peter got out of the boat and walked on the water and came to Jesus. But when he saw the wind, he was afraid, and beginning to sink, he cried out, 'Lord, save me.' Jesus immediately reached out his hand and took hold of him..." This passage about Peter walking on water emphasizes the importance of faith in stepping out of one's comfort zone and trusting in God, even in challenging circumstances.

Group Discussion

How do Andrea's experiences, including the physical challenges she faced and the falling out with her friends, relate to the concept of faith and the role it plays in our lives? Can you think of instances in your life where challenges and hardships have tested your faith, and how did you navigate them?

Andrea talks about the importance of faith and how it has been a constant source of strength and guidance. In your own life, how has faith helped you face moments of desperation and uncertainty? Can you share a personal experience where faith played a crucial role in finding a way through challenging times?

Andrea mentions her unexpected meeting with her husband and how it changed the trajectory of her life. How do you see God's providence at work in moments when unexpected and seemingly unrelated events come together to shape our lives for the better? Have you had similar experiences where God's plan became evident in hindsight?
