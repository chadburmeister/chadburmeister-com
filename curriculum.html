Be Extraordinary Groups

The Curriculum.

Brace yourself for an epic journey with Be Extraordinary Groups, where you'll witness firsthand the powerful stories of people just like you — people who've faced down the giants in their lives and emerged stronger. Real challenges, real transformation. You'll be right there with our guests as they recount those critical "buzz saw" moments and share the undeniable times when God stepped in.

"For everything that was written in the past was written to teach us, so that through endurance and the encouragement of the Scriptures we might have hope."
— Romans 15:4

How It Works

Weekly meet-up instructions.

Watch as many interviews as you can before each weekly meet-up. Ask each group member if there are any prayer requests for the week, and open each group meeting with a prayer. Decide as a group which conversation you'd like to focus on, then watch, listen, and participate in the conversation. Read the Bible verses that relate to the conversation you just heard, review and discuss the group questions, and play the song at the end. Go in peace, serve the Lord!
