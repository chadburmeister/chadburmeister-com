Be Extraordinary Groups · Week 3

Kris Dehnert

#PRIORITIES · 4:11

▶  Watch and Listen

Summary

Kris Dehnert shares a pivotal moment in his life that helped him shift his perspective and prioritize what truly matters. He recounts the time when he ruptured his appendix but delayed seeking medical attention due to his busy schedule and misplaced priorities. This experience made him realize the importance of focusing on the most significant aspects of life, such as family and personal well-being. It prompted him to make changes in his life, including delegating tasks, outsourcing, and designing his work-life balance so that he could spend more time with his children.

Faith also plays a significant role in Dehnert's journey. He acknowledges that he didn't achieve his success on his own and believes in the power of prayer and divine intervention. He expresses gratitude every night and has developed an attitude of gratitude, which has positively impacted his life. Overall, this transcript highlights the importance of facing challenges, shifting perspectives, and embracing gratitude in order to live a better and more fulfilling life.

Relevant Bible Verses

Matthew 6:33 - "But seek first his kingdom and his righteousness, and all these things will be given to you as well."

This verse reflects Kris's shift in priorities, putting the most important things in life, like family and personal well-being, before work or business.

1 Timothy 6:17 - "Command those who are rich in this present world not to be arrogant nor to put their hope in wealth, which is so uncertain, but to put their hope in God, who richly provides us with everything for our enjoyment."

This verse underscores Kris's realization that true wealth lies in experiences, not just material success.

Psalm 107:1 - "Give thanks to the Lord, for he is good; his love endures forever."

Kris's practice of gratitude and thanksgiving in every moment aligns with the theme of this verse.

Proverbs 3:5-6 - "Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight."

Kris's acknowledgment that he didn't achieve his success on his own and his faith in divine intervention resonate with the message in these verses.

Matthew 11:28 - "Come to me, all you who are weary and burdened, and I will give you rest."

Kris's transformative experience of realizing the need to shift from a busy and exhausting life to one where he can have meaningful experiences aligns with the idea of finding rest and peace in God.

Group Discussion

How can we proactively assess our priorities and ensure that we are focusing on the most important aspects of life, especially in the face of busy schedules and career demands, as discussed in Kris Dehnert's story?

In Kris's journey, faith and gratitude played a significant role in helping him navigate life's challenges. How can we incorporate daily practices of faith and gratitude to enhance our well-being and interactions with others in the future?

Kris Dehnert talked about the importance of reevaluating his approach to work and life balance. How can we apply the idea of delegating, outsourcing, automating, and reprioritizing our lives to create a healthier balance and make more time for experiences and meaningful moments in our own future endeavors?
