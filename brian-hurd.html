Be Extraordinary Groups · Week 2

Brian Hurd

#FAITH · 8:31

▶  Watch and Listen

Summary

Chad Burmeister interviews Brian Hurd about the challenges he has faced and the role of faith in his life. Brian shares how his father leaving at age seven initially rocked his world, but ultimately led him to seek a relationship with God. He talks about how other men stepped in as father figures, shaping his life and leading him to forgiveness and gratitude. Brian also recounts a time when his youngest son faced health issues, and how it strengthened his faith as he entrusted his son's life to God. Overall, Brian emphasizes the importance of stepping into the unknown with faith and trusting in God's plan, as well as the transformative power of forgiveness and gratitude.

Appropriate Bible Verses

Ephesians 4:32 (NIV) - "Be kind and compassionate to one another, forgiving each other, just as in Christ God forgave you." (Relevant to the theme of forgiveness and letting go of past hurts)

Romans 8:28 (NIV) - "And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose." (Relevant to finding purpose in challenging circumstances)

James 1:2-4 (NIV) - "Consider it pure joy, my brothers and sisters, whenever you face trials of many kinds because you know that the testing of your faith produces perseverance. Let perseverance finish its work so that you may be mature and complete, not lacking anything." (Relevant to facing and growing through challenges)

2 Corinthians 1:3-4 (NIV) - "Praise be to the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of compassion and the God of all comfort, who comforts us in all our troubles so that we can comfort those in any trouble with the comfort we ourselves receive from God." (Relevant to finding comfort and using personal experiences to help others)

Proverbs 3:5-6 (NIV) - "Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight." (Relevant to trusting in God's plan and guidance in challenging times)

Group Discussion

Forgiveness and Gratitude: Brian Hurd's story highlights the power of forgiveness and gratitude in transforming personal challenges into opportunities for growth. Can you share a similar experience where forgiveness or gratitude played a significant role in your life, and how did it impact your journey?

Stepping into the Unknown: Brian mentions the importance of stepping into the unknown with faith, trusting in God's plan. What challenges or opportunities in your life have required you to take a leap of faith, and how did it shape your journey and relationship with God?

Supporting Others: Brian's story also underscores the significance of mentors and supportive figures in our lives. Have you been a mentor or provided support to someone facing challenges? How did this experience affect both you and the person you helped, and what role did faith play in these relationships?
