Be Extraordinary Groups · Week 3

Beth Fisher

#LEUKEMIA · 8:48

▶  Watch and Listen

Summary

In this transcript, Beth Fisher shares her personal journey of facing unexpected challenges and finding strength and resilience to overcome them. After going through a divorce and being diagnosed with leukemia at a young age, Beth navigated the difficulties of being a single mother while battling a life-threatening illness. She emphasizes the importance of gratitude and being present in the moment, as well as the transformative power of faith in her life. Despite facing societal expectations and religious rules, Beth discovered a deeper understanding of grace and mercy, realizing that her relationship with God is not defined by man-made regulations but by a personal connection.

Throughout her experiences, Beth highlights the significance of having an experiential understanding of hardships in order to offer support and guidance to others. She shares how her faith played a central role in her journey, providing her with comfort, strength, and a sense of purpose. Beth's story serves as a reminder of the importance of resilience, gratitude, and the profound impact that faith can have in navigating life's challenges.

Group Discussion

Question on Finding Strength in Adversity:

Discussion Question: Beth faced an unexpected divorce and a leukemia diagnosis at a young age. She expressed the importance of gratitude and being present in the moment. Can you share a challenging life event you've experienced and how it changed your perspective on what truly matters in life?

Relevant Bible Verse: Romans 8:28 (NIV) - "And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose."

Application to Life: Discuss how adversity can be a catalyst for personal growth and a shift in priorities, as highlighted in the provided Bible verse. How can the group apply the principle of finding strength in adversity and recognizing God's purpose in challenging circumstances to their own lives?

Question on Grace, Mercy, and Relationship with God:

Discussion Question: Beth shared a moment when she felt an undeniable connection with God when confronted with religious rules. Have you ever encountered a situation where man-made rules conflicted with your relationship with God? How did you navigate this challenge, and what did you learn from it?

Relevant Bible Verse: Ephesians 2:8-9 (NIV) - "For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith—and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God—not by works, so that no one can boast."

Application to Life: Explore the concept of grace, mercy, and the relationship with God, as emphasized in the provided Bible verse. Discuss how recognizing God's grace can help individuals navigate conflicts between religious rules and a personal relationship with God.

3. Question on the Role of Faith in Daily Life:

Discussion Question: Beth expressed the vital role that faith plays in her daily life, describing it as her baseline for facing life's challenges. How does faith provide you with strength, guidance, and a sense of purpose in your daily life? Are there specific Bible verses or teachings that resonate with you in your faith journey?

Relevant Bible Verse: Proverbs 3:5-6 (NIV) - "Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your understanding; in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight."

Application to Life: Discuss the significance of faith as a source of strength and guidance, as described in the provided Bible verse. How can the group apply the principle of trusting in the Lord and seeking His guidance in their daily lives?
