Be Extraordinary Groups · Week 4

Kevin McCarthy

#I WILL FOLLOW · 6:36

▶  Watch and Listen

Summary

Kevin McCarthy shares his journey of finding faith in his early 30s and how it transformed his life. Growing up in a household where his father was Catholic and his mother rejected the church, Kevin didn't have a strong religious upbringing. However, after getting married, he and his wife decided to attend a church and Kevin realized the profound wisdom and truth in the Bible. His faith became an intellectual understanding in 1984 and then a heartfelt experience in 1986. Despite facing financial challenges in a business divorce, Kevin found solace and abundance in his spiritual faith, recognizing that his treasure was no longer in money but in Christ. This experience strengthened his faith and became a pivotal moment in his spiritual development.

Today, Kevin cannot imagine his life without faith and emphasizes the importance of forgiveness, reconciliation, and finding purpose through a relationship with Christ. He believes that having faith and living a life of hope and meaning is the greatest gift, and his mission is to help others discover their purpose in life and live it out to the fullest. For Kevin, faith is not just a religious concept but a transformative force that brings fulfillment and allows individuals to express their true selves as God intended.

Kevin's journey of coming to Christ in his early 30s and the subsequent transformative effect of faith aligns with the idea of trusting in the Lord's plan.

Colossians 3:2 (NIV) - "Set your minds on things above, not on earthly things."

Kevin's story of transitioning from a focus on material wealth to spiritual abundance reflects the importance of setting one's mind on things above.

Jeremiah 29:11 (NIV) - "For I know the plans I have for you, declares the Lord, plans for welfare and not for evil, to give you a future and a hope."

This verse relates to Kevin's mission of helping people discover their purpose and find meaning in their lives through faith.

Philippians 4:13 (NIV) - "I can do all this through him who gives me strength."

Group Discussion

How have you personally experienced a transformation in your life, shifting from a focus on material wealth or worldly concerns to living a life centered around faith and God's glory?

Can you share a significant moment in your life when you had to trust in the Lord's plan and submit to His guidance, even when it may not have aligned with your own understanding? How did this experience impact your life's direction?

In your journey to live a life focused on spiritual abundance and godly principles, have you noticed any commonalities or similarities among those you've encountered or interviewed? What aspects of faith, transformation, or purpose do you believe unite these individuals on their paths?
