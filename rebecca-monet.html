Be Extraordinary Groups · Week 2

Rebecca Monet

#DIVORCE · 4:50

▶  Watch and Listen

Summary

Rebecca describes how her parents' divorce and her father's loss of career as a pastor deeply affected her and her family. She explains that she coped by suppressing her emotions and not asking questions, but the pain still affects her today. However, she has used this experience to shape her business, helping others find their true purpose and avoid similar traumas. Rebecca also discusses the role of faith in her life and business, stating that she turns to scriptures for guidance and sees herself as a model of Christ's teachings in her daily life.

In summary, Rebecca shares a traumatic experience from her childhood and how it has influenced her business and perspective on purpose. She also emphasizes the importance of faith in guiding her decisions and being an example of Christ's teachings.

Group Discussion

Question on Coping with Trauma and Adversity:

Discussion Question: Rebecca shared a painful memory of her parents' divorce and her father's loss of his career as a pastor, which had a profound impact on her. Have you experienced a similar period of adversity or trauma in your life that significantly shaped your beliefs or path? How did you cope with it at the time, and how do you see it influencing your life or decisions today?

Relevant Bible Verse: Psalm 34:17-18 (NIV) - "The righteous cry out, and the Lord hears them; he delivers them from all their troubles. The Lord is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit."

Application to Life: Discuss the provided Bible verse and how faith can provide solace and guidance during challenging times. Share personal stories of overcoming adversity and how they have impacted your beliefs and choices.

Question on Purpose and Impact:

Discussion Question: Rebecca mentioned that her business is focused on helping people find their true purpose and talents to avoid experiences like her father's. How do you perceive the role of life's challenges and experiences in shaping your sense of purpose? Have you ever felt a calling to help others based on your personal history or skills?

Relevant Bible Verse: Romans 8:28 (NIV) - "And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose."

Application to Life: Explore the concept of purpose and how God's plan may be at work even in difficult situations, as suggested in the provided Bible verse. Share insights into how your experiences have influenced your life's mission and helped you make a positive impact.

3. Question on Faith as a Guiding Compass:

Discussion Question: Rebecca mentioned that she relies on scriptures as her roadmap in her business, turning to them for answers in various situations. How do you incorporate your faith into your daily life and decision-making processes? What role does seeking guidance from spiritual teachings play in your personal and professional journey?

Relevant Bible Verse: Proverbs 3:5-6 (NIV) - "Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your understanding; in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight."

Application to Life: Discuss the significance of trusting in God's guidance, as suggested in the provided Bible verse. Share examples of how your faith has influenced your decision-making and how it has helped you navigate life's challenges.
